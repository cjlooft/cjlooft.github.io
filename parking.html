Parking Lot Rules (for Drivers)
part 1

1. One-Way lanes.
Drive in the RIGHT (or left) direction

2. Speed = less than 10 MPH ...and 5 MPH in parking lanes.

3. STOP at stop signs. - don't just slow down. - it doesn't matter if no one is in the parking lot -

4. Pedestrians (people) have the "right-of-way" while walking in the crosswalk.

Parking Lot Rules (for Drivers)
part 2

1. Cell phone
Stay off the phone. - or use "hands-free" calling

2. Don't swerve in the lanes to avoid speed-bumps

3. STOP at stop signs. - don't just slow down.
- if people are in the crosswalk and you don't stop/yield there could be serious consequences -

4. Only drop off and pick up at designated areas.
- Do you know where these areas are? while walking in the crosswalk.

5. Don't back into parking spaces -that's the rule!
